How It Works
KNOW YOUR
NUMBER BEFORE
YOU TRADE.

The consistency rule means your best single trading day cannot represent more than a set % of your total profits. This calculator does the math instantly.

1

Select your account type & size

Different funded account programs have different consistency limits — 20%, 35%, or 40%. Pick yours to get the right threshold.

2

Enter each day's profit or loss

Type in what you made (or lost) each trading day. Losses count against your total, tightening the rule.

3

See your consistency score instantly

The calculator shows you Best Day ÷ Total Earnings. Green means you're passing. Red means one of your days is too large — adjust before you request a payout.

4

Use it inside the desktop app too

The full FuturesCalc desktop app has this built in alongside the tick-to-USD converter — all in one compact, beautiful window.
