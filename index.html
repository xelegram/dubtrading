FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does the bot guarantee passing the evaluation?+
No bot can guarantee results, but DubTrading is designed to maximize the odds by strictly following each firm's rules. Our historical pass rate is 94% on active accounts.
Does it work with any prop firm?+
DubTrading supports the main firms: Apex Trader Funding, TopStep, Tradeify, Earn2Trade, TopTier and more. Each preset includes the correct parameters for each firm.
Do I need to keep my computer on?+
For NinjaTrader the platform must stay open. For Tradovate we offer cloud hosting (Premium and Elite plans) so the bot runs 24/5 without depending on your machine.
Can I use my own strategies?+
Yes, on the Elite plan you can fully customize parameters or integrate your own signals. Standard and Premium include pre-configured, tested strategies.
What markets does the bot trade?+
DubTrading primarily trades index futures (ES, NQ, MES, MNQ), energy (CL) and metals (GC). Available instruments depend on the platform and selected preset.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes. All plans include a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. If you are not satisfied, simply don't continue.
